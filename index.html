Chris Lane

The voice behind "Take Back Home", "For Her" and "Big Big Plans!"

Early Life and Career Beginnings

  • Born and raised in Kernersville, North Carolina he is one of two twins.
  • He played footbal and baseball throughout Glenn High School and the Univeristy of North Carolina in Charlotte.
  • Chris learned to play the guitar when he realised his sustained injuries affected his chance at a professional baseball career.
  • While living at home, Chris worked for his fathers landscaping business and started a cover band which played in High Point University cafeteria.
  • In 2007, Chris and his brother auditioned for the 7th season of American Idol, but did not make it to Hollywood.
  • In 2008, Seth England of Big Loud Mountain management met Chris and lauched his career as a country music artist.

    • Career

  • Before persuing a solo career, Chris was the front man for the Chris Lane Band who's album Let's Ride charted on the Billboard Top Country Albums chart in 2012.
  • In 2013, Chris moved to Nashville, TN and opened for The Band Perry, Eli Young Band and many others.
  • On June 10, 2014, Chris' debut single, "Broken Windshield View" was relased to country radio by Big Loud Mountain. It sold 11,000 downloads in the first week.
  • In 2017, Chris was nominated for iHeartRadio Music Awards "Best New Country Artist" and ACM Awards "New Male Vocalist of the Year".

    • Personal Life

  • Chris started dating Lauren Bushnell, the winner of the 20th season of The Bachelor in 2018, got engaged on June 16th 2019 and married in Nashhville on October 25th 2019.
  • The both live happily ever after in Nashville, and Chris still makes some of the best country music today.
